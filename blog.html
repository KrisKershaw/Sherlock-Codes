THE DAY IS HERE, THE RELEASE OF OUR WIDELY ANTICIPATED GAME - CODE-BLOODED KILLER! Putting a name to the face - November 2020

Celebrations to be had, our game is now into it's beta phase! At this point the game requires significant testing and tweeking, but the horizon isn't far away for the release of our game! Oh, the name you ask? Well we've got one for you. Drum roll please... Code-Blooded Killer! We'll also drop a little bit more and give you a premise: Awoken in the early hours of the morning by a phone call, you - the detective - are told to immediately report down to the scene of a crime... a brutal murder. With puzzles, evidence and conundrums to be explored, will you be able to figure out who committed the murder? Love, the Sherlock Codes team.
