Who is Sherlock Codes?:



Sherlock Codes is currently a small indie games developer that pride themselves on creating and inventing fun projects that we ourselves would love to play!



We first started out in July 2020 with the premise of an idea for a murder mystery game. Things have been hectic ever since, but we're extremely proud to be able to say we've finished our first ever game: Code-Blooded Killer!



Our team of 4 will be looking to expand in the future - if you feel like you'd want to join our family here at Sherlock Codes, please don't hesitate to e-mail us on our Contact page! We're not currently hiring but will definitely be looking into expanding our team in the near future.
