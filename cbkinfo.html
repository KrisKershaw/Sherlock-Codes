About the game:

Cold-Blooded killer is a murder mystery game created by us, Sherlock Codes!
You play as the detective, awoken in the early hours by a phone call alerting you to a murder that's taken place in the headquarters of cyber-security company Rogue Nation.
You find Jason, the leader of a small group of coders, murdered via stapler.

Will you be able to figure out who was the murderer, or will you send down an innocent suspect as the killer walks free?
